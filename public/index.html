About Us

Welcome to DIAT,DRDO

The Defence Institute of Advanced Technology, (DIAT) as it is known today, came into being an Institute of Armament Studies in 1952 in the CME campus. In 1967, the Institute was renamed as "Institute of Armament Technology, (IAT)", which moved to its present location at Girinagar,Pune. From the relatively narrow scope of Armament Studies alone in the fifties, the role of the Institute was considerably enlarged by the Defence R&D Council in 1964 and further in 1981.

On the basis of the accreditation by the All India Councli of Technical Education (AICTE), Pune UNiversity recognised eight courses for the award of ME degree in 1980. In the year 2000, the Institute accquired the status of a Deemed to be a University. IAT has been renamed as DIAT w.e.f. 1st April 2006.
